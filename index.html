Pioneering the Future of Air Mobility

Founded in 2019, Air Mobility emerged from a vision to revolutionize urban logistics through autonomous aerial technology. What started as a research project at MIT has grown into a leading force in commercial drone delivery.

Today, we operate in 45 cities across North America and Europe, completing over 250,000 deliveries annually. Our commitment to safety, sustainability, and innovation drives everything we do.
